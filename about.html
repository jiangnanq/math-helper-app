About Math Helper App

Purpose

The Math Helper App is designed to assist primary and secondary school students in Singapore who struggle with mathematics. Our goal is to enhance their skills and confidence in solving math problems.

How It Works

This app provides tools to help students solve word problems and generate similar problems for practice. By using our app, students can master math concepts through repetition and guided learning.

Features

  • Step-by-step solutions to word problems
  • Problem generation for practice
  • User-friendly interface
  • Progress tracking
